Catálogo Hacia un lugar común

A project consisting of six paintings of sinking ships, a couple more with fragments of shipwrecks, a video- performance where I sing sixty melodious pop ballads from previous decades, an installation with diverse objects piled up in a storage room, and a personal collection of drawings and prints with nautical motives by different people.
