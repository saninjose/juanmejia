Untitled (The unknown masterpiece)

Transcripción completa a mano sobre la pared de La obra maestra desconocida de Balzac
2002

“Este lugar palpita, pero aquél está inmóvil; la vida y la muerte luchan en cada fragmento: aquí es una mujer, allí una estatua, mas allá un cadáver. Tu creación está incompleta. Sólo has podido insuflar una porción de tu alma en tu querida obra.”

–Honoré de Balzac

Complete transcription of The Unknown Masterpiece by Balzac
2002

“Here the pulse seems to beat, there it is motionless, life and death are at strife in every detail; here you see a woman, there a statue, there again a corpse. Your creation is incomplete. You had only power to breathe a portion of your soul into your beloved work.”

–Honoré de Balzac
